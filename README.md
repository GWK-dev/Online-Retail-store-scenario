# Online-Retail-store-scenario
🏢 COMPANY: SwiftCart Logistics
Role: Junior Data Analyst
Task: 4-Week Monthly Performance Report
You receive DAILY data for 4 weeks.
Your job:
•	Analyze each week individually
•	Then compile a full month report
________________________________________
📁 DATASETS (Daily Data Per Week)
Each week has 7 days.
________________________________________
YOUR TASKS
PART A — Weekly Analysis (For Each Week)
For each week calculate:
1.	Total weekly revenue
2.	Total weekly deliveries
3.	Total weekly orders
4.	Average daily revenue
5.	Delivery completion rate
(total deliveries ÷ total orders × 100)
6.	Complaint rate
(total complaints ÷ total orders × 100)
________________________________________
PART B — Monthly Analysis
After analyzing each week:
1.	Total monthly revenue
2.	Best performing week (revenue)
3.	Worst performing week (revenue)
4.	Overall monthly completion rate
5.	Overall monthly complaint rate
6.	Revenue growth from Week 1 → Week 4
